### Item 4c. Pull Request #109

**Motion:** By MVJ to amend Rule 209 and rename it to Rule 409.  
**Seconded by:** OK  


#### First Amendment
**Moved by:** JH  
**Amendment:** to add You can't resubmit in order to to get higher score 
**Action:** Withdrawn by mover. *(No vote taken.)*


#### Second Amendment
**Moved by:** MVJ  
**Amendment:** To add “rounded to the nearest integer” to the last sentence.  
**Vote:**  
Aye – MVJ, JH, HR, JD, IJ, OK, CN  
Nay – None  
**Result:** Amendment carried.

#### Third Amendment
**Moved by:** JH  
**Amendment:** To insert *“taking the square root, multiplying by 16,”* **after “reading off the bottom byte,”** in the same sentence.
**Vote:**  
Aye – MVJ, JH, HR, JD, OK  
Nay – IJ, CN  
**Result:** Amendment carried.

**Final Motion (as amended):**  
Rule 209 is amended and renamed **Rule 409**, with the revised sentence reading as follows:

> “A player scores points computed by taking the Git hash of the first commit in the pull request, reading off the bottom byte, **taking the square root, multiplying by 16,** and multiplying the resulting number by the fraction of votes in favor of the rule change, **rounded up to the nearest integer.**”

**Vote:**  
Aye – MVJ, JH, IJ, CN, JD, OK  
Nay – HR

**Result:** Motion carried.
