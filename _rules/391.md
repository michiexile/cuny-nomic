mutability: mutable
short_name: Clarity Bonus
---

To encourage clarity and efficiency in rule-making:  

1. Every new rule shall include a short descriptive name, in addition to its number, for quick reference.  
2. If a proposal is adopted in the exact form in which it was originally submitted, without any amendments made during debate, the proposer shall receive an additional 15 points.
